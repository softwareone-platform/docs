# View billing charges

Your billing statement lists all charges for a billing period.&#x20;

These charges are used to generate your invoice and correspond to subscription-related transactions in your agreement. You can review each charge to understand how your total invoice is calculated.

### Viewing charges

You can view the charges for a statement in two ways:

* **In the Excel Billing Statement (.xlsx)** - The **Charges** tab lists all individual charges included in the statement.
* **In the Marketplace Platform** - The **statement details** page displays all individual charges. &#x20;

To view the charges in the platform:

1. Go to **Billing** > **Statements**.
2. (Optional) Refine your list of statements by applying filters. For details, see [Filter billing statements](filter-billing-statements.md).
3. Find the statement you want, then select the link in the **ID** column. The statement details page opens.
4. On the **Charges** tab, review the individual charges. To view the extended information for a specific charge, select **View details** in the **Actions** column.
