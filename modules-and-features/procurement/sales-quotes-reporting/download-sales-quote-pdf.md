# Download sales quote PDF

You can download a PDF version of your sales quote from the quote details page in the SoftwareOne Marketplace.&#x20;

### Downloading a sales quote as a PDF

To download a sales quote as a PDF:

1. Go to **Procurement** > **Sales quotes reporting**.&#x20;
2. (Optional) Use filters to find the desired quote.
3. Select the quote ID. The details page of the sales quote opens.
4. Select the **Attachments** tab.

<div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (19).png" alt=""><figcaption><p align="center">Use the Attachments tab to download the sales quote PDF.</p></figcaption></figure></div>

5. Complete one of the following steps to download the PDF:
   * Select the PDF link in the **Reference** column.
   * Under **Actions**, select the more icon (**•••**), then choose **Download**.&#x20;
