# Mobile App FAQs

### General Access

<details>

<summary>From where can I get the app?</summary>

The app is available by invitation only. If you have received an invitation, follow the link in your email to download the app from the appropriate app store.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I complete the account registration process using the app after my account administrator has invited me?</summary>

If your account administrator has invited you to the SoftwareOne Marketplace, you must register and complete the sign-up process using the web version of the platform: [https://portal.platform.softwareone.com/](https://portal.platform.softwareone.com/).&#x20;

Once your account is set up, you can use the app to access your account if you have been invited to use the app.

</details>

### Device & System Requirements

<details>

<summary>What devices does the app support?</summary>

The app is optimized for iOS mobile devices with a screen size < 7”, but it works on tablets and larger devices.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What version of iOS is supported? </summary>

The app supports iOS version 17 and later.

</details>

### App Functionality

<details>

<summary>Can I purchase subscriptions and add or remove licenses using the app?</summary>

The app only supports a limited set of features optimized for mobile use.&#x20;

You cannot place orders or modify subscriptions through the app. To view the list of supported features, see [App capabilities](app-overview/#app-capabilities).

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I update my profile through the app?</summary>

Currently, the app only supports read-only actions. Therefore, it's not possible to update your profile or other account settings.&#x20;

To view the list of supported features, see [App capabilities](app-overview/#app-capabilities).

</details>

<details>

<summary>Which languages does the app support?</summary>

The app supports English only. Additional languages are planned for a future release.

</details>

### Sign-in & Security

<details>

<summary>How do I sign in to the app?</summary>

Use your existing SoftwareOne Marketplace account and the verification code sent to your registered email address.&#x20;

For enhanced security, the app uses passwordless authentication. Therefore, you don't need to enter your password to sign in.

</details>

<details>

<summary>My verification code is no longer valid.</summary>

Verification codes are only valid for 3 minutes. If your code is no longer valid, use the **Resend code** option during sign-in to generate a new one.

</details>

<details>

<summary>I have trouble signing in to the app.</summary>

If you are having trouble signing in to the app, try these troubleshooting steps:

* Check your internet connection.
* Ensure you are using the email address you provided when you set up your Marketplace account.
* Make sure that your account is active. If you haven't accepted your account invitation yet, you must do so and complete the registration first through the web version at [https://portal.platform.softwareone.com/](https://portal.platform.softwareone.com/).
* If you are not receiving the verification code, check your Spam or Junk email folders. You can also resend the code.
* Make sure that you are entering a valid verification code. Verification codes are only valid for 3 minutes.

If the issue persists, [contact support](../help-and-support/contact-support.md) for assistance.

</details>

### Data & Troubleshooting

<details>

<summary>What data do you collect?</summary>

We collect your unique user ID, which helps us identify your account, and your device ID.&#x20;

Additionally, we gather usage data and diagnostic information, including crash and performance data, to improve our services.

</details>

<details>

<summary>I noticed a discrepancy in the information within the mobile app.</summary>

If you notice any discrepancy, we recommend checking the web version of the platform.

Sometimes, temporary issues can cause discrepancies. The web version reflects the most accurate and complete data.

</details>
