# Alga PSA

[Alga PSA](https://www.nineminds.com/alga-psa) is an open‑source Professional Services Automation (PSA) platform that focuses on Managed Service Provider (MSP) scenarios, including service desk/support, project management, billing and invoicing, and more.&#x20;

Marketplace Platform's integration with Alga PSA simplifies day-to-day operations for partners and helps them scale their subscription resale business more effectively. It allows partners to manage their business within a single platform by bringing subscription and agreement data from the Marketplace directly into their PSA environment. This removes the need to switch between multiple systems and streamlines service management, customer administration, and billing processes.&#x20;

Partners can also manage Marketplace subscriptions alongside their existing customer and service workflows. The integration also supports a partner-led model that allows partners to provide their customers with controlled access to their subscriptions, while still maintaining ownership of the customer relationship and billing.

### Key features <a href="#key-benefits" id="key-benefits"></a>

With the SoftwareOne Marketplace and Alga PSA integration, you can:

* **Connect Alga PSA to SoftwareOne Marketplace** - Use the SoftwareOne extension to establish a secure connection between both platforms.&#x20;
* **Sync agreements and subscriptions into Alga PSA** - Bring agreements and subscriptions from SoftwareOne Marketplace into Alga PSA, and get full visibility within your PSA environment.
* **Map Marketplace agreements to customer service** - Link synced agreements to the relevant customer and service records in Alga PSA, and manage Marketplace subscriptions within your own service delivery model.
* **Apply your own markup** - Add markup to agreements to support your resale model and align Marketplace subscriptions with your commercial approach.
* **Control customer visibility** - Decide whether a subscription or agreement is hidden, visible, or available through self-service.
* **Provide customer portal access** - Enable your customers to view items you have made available to them through the Alga PSA client portal, including agreements, subscriptions, orders, and statements.
* **Enable limited self-service for customers** - Allow your customers to manage certain subscriptions themselves while retaining the overall service relationship.
* **Manage subscriptions directly from Alga PSA** - Handle subscription updates directly in Alga PSA, without switching between different systems.
* **Support order-related workflows** - Manage order-related processes, including required inputs and status tracking, to improve operational efficiency.
* **Automate billing activities** - Use synced agreements, statements, and orders to support automated billing processes, such as creating invoices.

### Getting started

SoftwareOne partners who use Alga PSA can install the SoftwareOne extension to link Alga PSA with the Marketplace Platform. For installation instructions, see [Install the SoftwareOne extension](install-the-softwareone-extension.md).

Once you have configured the extension, your Marketplace account details, along with information such as agreements, subscriptions, orders, and statements, will be accessible in Alga PSA.
