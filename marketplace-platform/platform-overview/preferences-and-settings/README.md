# Preferences and Settings

Preferences and settings help you customize your Marketplace Platform account, so you can work more efficiently across accounts and pages, and data views.&#x20;

Use the topics in this section to update your profile, change what you see in tables (data grids), control notifications, and get quick access to the pages you use most.
