# Create and View Orders

### Creating Orders <a href="#creating-orders" id="creating-orders"></a>

You can access the shopping cart in two ways.

Click on “Buy” in the main PyraCloud navigation and selecting “Shopping Cart”,

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-120-1024x427.png" alt="" height="427" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 1 – Shopping Cart Menu</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Or click on the Shopping Cart icon in the top right hand corner of the PyraCloud page.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-121-1024x104.png" alt="" height="104" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 2 – Shopping Cart Icon</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Once in the Shopping Cart, you will see the product(s) you have added to your cart. To create an order, simply click on “Create Order” as shown below:

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-122-1024x418.png" alt="" height="418" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 3 – Create Order</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

This will open the page shown below:

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-123-1024x602.png" alt="" height="602" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 4 – Create Order Page View</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Fill in all the details and click on “Create Order”.

This will open up the “Order Detail” page. This is the confirmation that the order is complete and sent to SoftwareONE.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/image-1024x514.png" alt="" height="514" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 5 – Order Details Page</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

### Orders History <a href="#orders-history" id="orders-history"></a>

Click on “Buy” from the main PyraCloud navigation bar and select “Orders”.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-124-1024x595.png" alt="" height="595" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 6 – Orders Menu Tab</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

This will take you to the “Orders” page which will show a list of orders that have been created.

Use the search and filter options to narrow down your results.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-125-1024x599.png" alt="" height="599" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 7 – Orders Page</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

The search can be customized by filtering the document by date or entering the PO Number in the search section on the top.

You can also customize the columns in the order list by clicking on “Customize” on the top right which will open the list of all available columns.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/image-1-1024x526.png" alt="" height="526" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 8 – Customizing Columns</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

All columns that are currently displayed have checkmarks next to their names. Tick the check mark against the columns that you would like to display and untick the check mark against the columns that you would like to hide.

In order to have these customizations available on next login, Click on “Save Configurations”

To change the order of the columns drag a column’s header and drop it to a new position.

In order to get a detailed view of the order you can simply click on the line item to be taken to the detailed page view. You can also click on “View” in the “Actions” column.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/image-2-1024x250.png" alt="" height="250" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 9 – Action – View</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Clicking on details will open up the below page providing details of the order.

The Order Details page will show all the products in the order. To view more product details, click on “View” in the “Actions” column.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/image-3-1024x476.png" alt="" height="476" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 10 – View More Product Details</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Clicking on “View” will display the product details as shown below:

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/image-4-1024x485.png" alt="" height="485" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 11 – Product Details Page View</strong></p></figcaption></figure>
