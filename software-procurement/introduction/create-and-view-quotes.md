# Create and View Quotes

### Creating a Quote <a href="#creating-a-quote" id="creating-a-quote"></a>

Access the shopping cart by either clicking on “Buy” in the main PyraCloud navigation bar and selecting “Shopping Cart”.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/pyc-shopping-cart-1024x492.jpg" alt="" height="492" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 1 – Shopping Cart Menu</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Or click on the Shopping Cart icon in the top right hand corner of the PyraCloud page.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-130-1024x104.png" alt="" height="104" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 2 – Shopping Cart Icon</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

To create a quote for the product in the cart, click on “Create Quote”.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-131-1024x353.png" alt="" height="353" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 3 – Create Quote</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Clicking on “Create Quote” will open up the below page:

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-132-1024x504.png" alt="" height="504" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 4 – Create Quote Page View</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Fill in the required details and click on “Create Quote” which will generate the quote instantly.

You will be navigated to the “Quote Details” page as shown below.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-133-1024x505.png" alt="" height="505" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 5 – Quote Details Page</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

### Quotes History <a href="#quotes-history" id="quotes-history"></a>

The “Quotes” section under the “Inventory” menu tab provides a list of all the quotes which have been generated.

To access the quotes click on the “Inventory” tab in the main PyraCloud navigation and select “Quotes”.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-134.png" alt="" height="787" width="913"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 6 – Quotes in Inventory Tab</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

This will take you to the Quotes Page.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-135-1024x505.png" alt="" height="505" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 7 – List of Quotes Generated</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Use the search to customize and filter your results.

#### Customizing Grid View <a href="#customizing-grid-view" id="customizing-grid-view"></a>

You can also customize the columns in the quote list.

On the top right hand side of the grid, click on “Customize”, this will open a list of all available columns.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/image-72-1024x324.png" alt="" height="324" width="1024"><figcaption><p><em><strong>Figure 8 – Customiz</strong></em><strong>ing the Grid View</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

All columns that are currently displayed have checkmarks next to their names. Tick the check mark against the columns that needs to be displayed and untick the check mark against the columns that you would like to hide.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/image-73-1024x352.png" alt="" height="352" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 9 – Selecting Columns to Display/Hide</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

In order to have these customizations available on next login, click on “Save Configurations”.

To change the order of the columns, drag a column header and drop it to a new position.

**Note:** The quotes for which the expiry date has already passed will be invalid. In such scenarios, a fresh quote needs to be created.

In order to get a detailed view of the quote, simply click on the line item and you will be taken to the Detailed Page view.

You can also click on the magnifying glass or 3 dots (…) in the “Action” column and then click on “Details”.

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/image-74-1024x216.png" alt="" height="216" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 10 – Action Column – Details</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

The “Quote Detail” page will show all the products in the quote. To view more product details, click on magnifying glass icon on right hand side as shown below:

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-137-1024x736.png" alt="" height="736" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 11 – More Details Icon</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

Clicking on the magnifying glass icon will display the product details as shown below:

<figure><img src="https://help.pyracloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/image-138-1024x593.png" alt="" height="593" width="1024"><figcaption><p><strong>Figure 12 – More Product Details Page View</strong></p></figcaption></figure>
